Folder | Source | What it is
iem_asos_1min | Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM) | ASOS 1-minute temperature data (~24h delay)
awc_metar | Aviation Weather Center (AWC) | METAR observations (hourly + SPECI)
iem_daily_climate | Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM) | NWS Daily Climate Report (CLI) - official high/low for Kalshi settlement
iem_metar_extracted | Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM) | Extracted temps from METAR text (run iem_metar_temp_extractor.py)
market_snapshots | Kalshi WebSocket | Market snapshots (yes_bid, yes_ask, last_price)
orderbook_snapshots | Kalshi WebSocket | Orderbook depth
synoptic_ws | Synoptic WebSocket | Real-time weather observations
weather_bot | Paper execution | Bot trade log

Download scripts: data/download/
  iem_asos_1min.py - IEM ASOS 1-min
  awc_metar.py - AWC METAR
  iem_daily_climate.py - IEM Daily Climate
  iem_metar_temp_extractor.py - IEM ASOS/METAR temp extraction
  run_weather_collection.py - Run all weather downloads
